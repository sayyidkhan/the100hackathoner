## What Deadline Pressure Teaches You

Forty-eight hours is long enough to make something real and short enough to remove excuses.

It teaches prioritisation because everything cannot survive. It teaches communication because confusion is expensive. It teaches humility because the demo always exposes the gap between the plan and the product.

Most of all, it teaches motion.

## The Lesson

You do not learn your way into shipping. You ship your way into learning.
